Other Studies Abot Why Women Likes to Date Older Men

So I looked for other studies that give a more detailed explanation of why men in the U.S. tend to date women whose younger than them. I checked the article Is this the real reason older men date younger women? Before reading the article, I thought the reason men prefer to date younger women is that younger women look "better". According to the author Kerri Sackville and her quote from Stacy London, "the reason women are devalued as they age is that we've(women) internalized the male gaze." As Kerri Sackville said "For a man, an older partner is a constant reminder of his own age. He cannot pretend he's still thirty when he's waking up next to a fifty-year-old woman. A younger partner is life-affirming. She helps to stave off his own fear of aging and mortality. If a man can wake up next to a woman a decade or two younger, he can convince himself that he is still young." So because women have 'internalized the male gaze' the opposite can be true for us. So according to Sackville "If our partner sees us as young and hot, we see ourselves as young and hot. If he sees us as aging and undesirable, we internalize that, too. A man is only as young as the woman he feels, but a woman is only as young as a man sees her be." Sackville's reasoning can apply to both older men and women in dating preferences. But they may not be the only reason. I think women, in general, like to date older men is it's because socially older men are more mature, more experienced, financially more stable. So women will like that part of older men if they ready to commit a relationship and start a family.

Reality TV Might Not Just Reflect Culture

TV shows like The Bachelor and The Bachelorette really did a great job in reflecting the dating culture in North America. But not all TV shows are just able to reflect the culture, some even capable of making a change. For example, a TV show like "13 Reasons Why" does not only reflect the cruel reality of the current society (suicide culture, bully culture, and rape culture, etc.) The production team of the show also uses their TV shows as a platform (13reasonswhy.info) to propagate the resources from other platforms to help people who suffered from the bully, rape, assault, etc. to find the useful lifesaving resources, and to prevent further damage to the victim's life.
